§10 · inherited behavior

The JS every page inherits

The reveal/stagger + count-up + parallax IntersectionObserver behavior lives here once. Inner pages ship NO JS — they only add the wow-* classes and inherit this. All motion is prefers-reduced-motion-gated (it falls back to fully-visible, no animation).

IntersectionObserver toggles .in-view on .wow-reveal / .wow-stagger (threshold .12, rootMargin -8%). .stat__num[data-count] counts up once at 50% visibility (easeOutCubic, ~1.5s). .wow-parallax translates on scroll (rAF-throttled, data-parallax speed). Reduced-motion → instant, no transforms.
