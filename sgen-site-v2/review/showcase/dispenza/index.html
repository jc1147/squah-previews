Why SGEN exists

Born from a real agency's demand.

By Dispenza's account, running a marketing agency at this scale exposed the same problem on every build: a tower of separate plugins, hosts, and logins, each one a thing to license, patch, and pray held together.

So the stack was built to replace all of it: one native platform where the builder, the hosting, the SEO, the menu and ecommerce, the forms and the analytics are one system on one login. It was proven across hundreds of live dispensary sites before it was ever offered to anyone else. The same platform that now ships every site in this showcase is the platform you can start on today.
