The whole lifecycle, native

From the field a visitor types in, to the lead in your inbox.

The Forms module owns six jobs in one place: build, protect, receive, report, export, and connect. No forms plugin to license, no spam service to subscribe to, no connector tier just to move a lead from the page to your tools.

Three ways to build

A Custom HTML code editor, a visual Canvas builder, and a multi-step Step Form, switch the template and the editor re-renders. One builder, three modes, not one rigid form.

Native reCAPTCHA

An enabled-reCAPTCHA toggle lives in Mail Settings. Turn it on and the forms you add later are covered automatically, with a per-form override when you need one. Spam protection is part of the module, not a separate subscription you renew.

A searchable leads inbox

Every submission lands in a built-in inbox you can filter by form, source, date, and status, search, bulk-action, and trash or restore. The lead data behind your numbers stops being a black box.

CSV and ZIP export

Export any filtered slice of entries, your fields plus the session columns, including a session-data-aware export. Forms themselves export to JSON, templates, mail, and destinations, and import back.

Lead reports, built in

A leads-by-source doughnut and a leads-over-time line, native to the module. You see which channel produces submissions without a separate analytics tool.

Reuse by shortcode

Each form carries a [myform id="N"] shortcode you drop on any page. Build the form once, place it anywhere, update it in one spot.

The stack it replaces

One module. The whole form stack, in the box.

SGEN Forms ships the builder, the spam gate, the inbox, the reports, and the integrations as one native module. Each of those is a separate product you would otherwise buy, license, and keep updated, a builder, a spam service, a connector add-on, each with its own renewal and maintainer. Questions

Frequently asked questions about Forms

Does SGEN Forms include spam protection? +
Yes, native reCAPTCHA through an enabled-reCAPTCHA toggle in Mail Settings. Connect your keys once and the forms you add later are covered, with a per-form override. Spam protection is part of the module, with no separate subscription to keep.
Can I route a submission to Slack, Airtable, or a webhook? +
Yes. Each form has its own Integrations panel with Slack, Airtable, and any webhook as live providers, plus Google Sheet coming soon. Destinations fire in order with per-field merge-tag mapping and a stop-after-first-success rule. Integrations are configured per form, not in a global hub, so each form can route to different destinations.
What data travels with each lead? +
Each entry carries its traffic source (Direct, Google Ads, Google Organic, Referral, Social, plus ChatGPT detection) and a per-lead session journey: landing page, referrer, UTMs, and a page-view timeline with time-on-page and scroll depth for every page the visitor touched.
Can I export form submissions? +
Yes. Export any filtered slice of entries to CSV or ZIP, your fields plus the session columns, including a session-data-aware export. Forms themselves export to JSON (templates, mail, metas, and destinations) and import back, so your forms and leads are portable.
Does SGEN support multi-step forms? +
Yes. Switch the template to Step Form to split a long survey or application across steps. Custom HTML and Canvas are the other two build modes, so you pick the right one per form.
What does the Connection Test actually do? +
It sends a one-time probe to verify a destination is reachable before you go live. Treat it as setup verification, not a repeating health check, because each probe sends a real message to the destination. It confirms the endpoint responds, the live delivery then happens on every submission.
