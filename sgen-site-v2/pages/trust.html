Compliance posture

Compliance-grade posture and audit-ready logging.

GDPR cookie consent with a per-session consent log for audit, native, not a third-party tag you bolt on and pray about. Posture, not a promise: a WAF in front, a consent banner that actually gates scripts, and a per-session log an auditor can read.

A consent banner you control: message, position (top, center, or bottom), buttons, and an optional require-a-checkbox mode for explicit consent.
Script gating: named trackers (Google Tag Manager, Microsoft Clarity, and others) plus any tracker matched by regex are held until the visitor accepts. Decline, and they never load.
Page exclusions: your privacy, cookie, and terms pages can be exempted so visitors read your policy without a banner over it.
A read-only consent log: every decision recorded with a timestamp, page, and accept or decline outcome. The audit trail itself, not a marketing dashboard.

Your consent records are an audit artifact you control, a per-decision trail you can hand an auditor on demand, not a slice of data held by a consent vendor you pay monthly. Whether your specific configuration satisfies a specific regulation is a question for your counsel, and we say so on the product itself.

Common questions

Straight answers on security and compliance.

Is SGEN HIPAA-compliant? +
Yes. SGEN is HIPAA compliant for healthcare. It runs on a single secure database with data protection and does not host patients' records or PHI, so the protected data stays under the clinic's control while the public site runs on a firewalled, centrally-patched platform.
Are you SOC 2 or ISO certified? +
Both are in progress. We do not display a certification badge and we do not use the word "certified," because we have not completed either yet. This page updates the moment that changes, and not before.
What is actually in the consent audit log? +
Each visitor decision is recorded with a timestamp, the page, and the accept-or-decline outcome, viewable as a read-only list and per-entry detail. It is an audit trail, not an analytics product.
Which plan includes the GDPR consent log and the WAF? +
The WAF is part of Foundation Pack, free on every plan including the free Sandbox. The Consent and Tracking module's consent banner and per-session log are native to the platform and included with the whole module set, on every plan.
Does SGEN comply with GDPR? +
SGEN gives you the tools: a consent banner that gates tracking scripts, page exclusions, and a per-session consent log, plus a Data Processing Agreement for processors. Whether your specific configuration satisfies GDPR is a determination for your counsel. We provide the mechanisms and the audit trail.
What security do I get without installing anything? +
The firewall and malware-protection layer, and login and access hardening, run at the platform level inside Foundation Pack and Users and Roles. They are native to the platform and included on every plan, so there is nothing to install, license, or keep updated.
Questions about our security posture?

A WAF, audit-ready logging, and a consent trail, on every plan.

All 23 modules and Foundation Pack are in every plan, priced by how many live sites you run: Indie at $39 per month (one site), Studio at $97 (three sites), Scale at $297 (ten sites), and Enterprise at $497 (unlimited). The free Sandbox runs everything on staging-only URLs, so you can build and protect a site before you pay anything.
