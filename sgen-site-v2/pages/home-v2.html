Answer 01 · Everything included

Yes. Every plan is the entire platform: all 23 native modules across 7 categories, plus the Foundation Pack of hosting, SSL, CDN, WAF, security patching, and the multi-site dashboard. That holds on the $0 Sandbox too, which runs the whole product on unlimited staging sites with no credit card and no time limit. Nothing is locked behind a higher tier, a suite, or an add-on. The only thing that changes as you move up a plan is how many live sites you run, never which features you get.

Answer 02 · Moving over

Whether you are on WordPress, Webflow, Wix, Squarespace, or another host, moving to SGEN is a rebuild, not a one-click import, and we will not pretend otherwise. Your content carries over: pages, posts, and media import through Advanced Import. The design is rebuilt on SG Builder, either by us as a white-glove project or by you with the builder and the templates library. You preview the whole site on a staging copy before anything goes live, and you land on one engine with nothing bolted on underneath, which is usually the reason people move.
