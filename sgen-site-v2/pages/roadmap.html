What is coming

What is coming to SGEN.

A working map of where the platform is headed: what already shipped, what is in progress, and what we are exploring. The roadmap is subject to change, and nothing moves into Shipped until it is live in your account.

We publish only what we can stand behind. We do not post dates we cannot commit to, and we do not list speculative items as if they were done. If it is in the Shipped column, it is on the changelog too.

The board

Shipped, in progress, exploring.

Three honest states. Shipped is live now and dated on the changelog. In progress is actively being built, with no committed date. Exploring is under research and may change or be dropped. All 23 modules are included on every plan, so anything that ships reaches every account.

Shipped
Live now

Already in your hands and dated on the changelog. A recent sample:

  • ✓Phone-tap call attribution. Click-to-call leads now carry the same traffic source as form submissions.
  • ✓Copy and paste sections across sites. Move a built section between SGEN sites with styling and images intact.
  • ✓Per-product inventory thresholds. Set low-stock states per product in the Ecommerce module.
  • ✓Scheduled publishing. Queue a staged change to go live at a chosen time.

See the full shipped record →

In progress
No date

Actively being built. These are real, but we have not set a date and will not until we are confident:

  • Deeper attribution reporting. Multi-touch source views that show every step a lead took before converting, building on the phone-tap logging already shipped.
  • More native integrations. A growing connector library so the modules you already run can pass data to the outside tools your business depends on.
  • Compliance-grade audit posture. Expanding audit-ready logging and access controls. SOC 2 and ISO work is in progress and tracked on the trust page, not claimed here as certified.
  • Builder performance pass. Faster canvas rendering on large multi-section pages.
Exploring
Research

Under research. Listed for honesty, not as a promise. Any of these may change shape or be dropped:

  • Reusable content blocks across a portfolio. Author a block once and reuse it across every site you manage.
  • Expanded localization. Deeper multi-language workflows inside the builder.
  • Richer storefront merchandising. More ways to group and surface products in the Ecommerce module.
Shipped

HIPAA compliance, for healthcare.

SGEN is HIPAA compliant for healthcare. It runs on a single secure database with data protection and does not host patients' records or PHI, so the protected data stays under the clinic's control while the public site runs on a firewalled, centrally-patched platform.

What is running today

The infrastructure layer healthcare operators ask about is included on every plan, including the free Sandbox:

  • ✓Web application firewall (WAF) on every site
  • ✓SSL and CDN, included at no extra charge
  • ✓Managed security patching
  • ✓Audit-ready logging
  • ✓Per-session consent logging from the Consent and Tracking module
