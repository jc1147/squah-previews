Why it pays back

You built the relationship. The platform should pay you back.

You did the discovery call, the scope, the trust. When the client picks a platform, that decision is worth something. On most platforms it is worth nothing to you, a one-time finder's fee that pays once and disappears, or nothing at all.

SGEN is built differently. Refer a client and you earn a recurring referral commission for as long as they stay on the platform. It costs nothing to join, and you can prove the platform before you pitch it: every partner gets a free Sandbox, the $0 plan, to build a real proof of concept on. You sell from a working build, not a slide deck.

Program benefits

Five things every partner gets on day one.

No tiers to climb before the program is worth your time. The moment you are approved, you have a free build environment, a public listing, and a recurring revenue line tied to the clients you bring.

Free to join

No application fee. No monthly access charge. No minimum referral quota to keep your status. You are a partner on your terms, for as long as you want to be.

Recurring referral commission

Most programs pay once, at signup, then forget you. SGEN pays on the relationship: every client you refer earns commission every month they stay. The relationship you already built keeps paying you.

A free Sandbox to build on

Every partner gets the Sandbox plan, which is $0, a real SGEN environment, not a watered-down demo. Build a real proof of concept on a client's actual content and demo it before anyone pays a thing.

A public directory listing

Your agency or practice appears in the SGEN partner directory, where SGEN users go to find a builder. The program sends work toward you: inbound lead flow, not just a commission line.

Partner tiers as you grow

The program has tiers, and they unlock additional benefits as your referral activity grows. The more you bring to the platform, the more the program gives back.

A platform worth recommending

Every plan is the whole platform: all 22 native modules, no plugin tax, no per-feature upsell. You recommend something you can stand behind, then you get paid for it.

The agency toolset

A real multi-site management layer, not a shared password.

Referring SGEN is the front of the relationship. Running your clients' sites is the rest of it, and this is where most platforms hand an agency a spreadsheet of logins and wish them luck.

SGEN gives partners a native multi-site layer: one dashboard for every client site, the ability to log in as a client without asking for their password, granular role-based access, and one-click cloning of your starter build. The "send me your password" support ticket disappears, and onboarding a new client takes seconds, not days.

Multi-site dashboard: every client site in one place, provision and monitor without ten separate logins.
Login-as-client: open any client's site as that client, no credentials handed over.
Role-based access control: scope exactly what each teammate or client contact can touch, no permissions plugin to license.
Bulk-clone: stand up a new client site from your template in seconds, your starter build replicated clean every time.

Multi-site management is part of the platform on every plan. The plans differ only in how many sites you run, at a published price: Studio (3 sites), Scale (10 sites), and Enterprise (unlimited). There is no separate add-on and no sales call to unlock the management layer. See pricing.

Questions

Partner program, straight answers

Is it really free to join? +
Yes. There is no application fee, no monthly membership charge, and no minimum referral quota to keep your partner status.
When do I start earning commission? +
Commission is recurring: once a client you referred is on a paid SGEN plan, you earn for as long as they stay, not just at signup. The exact terms are confirmed with you when you join.
How does the referral get tracked to me? +
You refer a client through the program, SGEN tracks the client back to your partner account, and your commission applies. The tracking method and terms are shared with you when you are approved.
What is the difference between the partner tiers? +
The program has tiers, and higher tiers unlock additional benefits as your referral activity grows. The tier structure is shared with you when you join the program.
What tools do I actually get as a partner? +
A free Sandbox (the free plan, a real environment to build and demo client projects on, across unlimited staging sites), a public directory listing, and the native agency toolset: multi-site dashboard, login-as-client, role-based access, and bulk-clone. The management layer is part of the platform on every plan.
Can I manage client sites under my own brand? +
You manage every client site today through the multi-site dashboard, login-as-client, role-based access, and bulk-clone. Those are live, confirmed partner tools. A named white-label feature is not something we announce on this page yet. When it is confirmed, we will say so here.
Who qualifies to become a partner? +
The program is open to agencies and freelancers who recommend SGEN to clients. There is no minimum team size. Submit the application and we will be in touch shortly.
Do my clients have to pay more because I referred them? +
No. Referring through the partner program does not change a client's price. They pay the same published plan pricing as anyone else.
