Pricing questions

Questions about plans and what's included

What's the difference between the plans? +
The number of live sites, not features. Every paid plan, Indie (1 site), Studio (3), Scale (10), Enterprise (unlimited), includes the entire platform: all 23 native modules, the Foundation Pack, and the full dashboard. The plans differ only by how many sites you can publish. The free Sandbox runs the whole platform too, on unlimited staging-only sites.
Is the free Sandbox feature-limited? +
No. The Sandbox runs the whole platform with every feature unlocked, on unlimited sandbox sites, at $0. The one limit is that Sandbox sites live on a SGEN staging URL, not your own domain. Build and evaluate everything first, or build client sites and hand them off, then upgrade to a paid plan to go live.
Do I have to buy suites or add-ons to unlock features? +
No. There are no suites and no à-la-carte add-ons. The whole platform ships in every plan, one stack, priced by site count. There's no plugin tax and no suite tax.
Can I really run unlimited sites on Enterprise, and is that the top plan? +
Yes. Enterprise is $497/mo (billed annually; $577 monthly) for an unlimited number of live sites, and it's the top plan, a published price you can check out on today, not a sales call.
Can I switch plans or cancel later? +
Yes. Cancel anytime, prorated, you pay only for what you've used. You can move up as you add sites. Because every plan has the same features, moving up only changes how many sites you run, never what you can build.
