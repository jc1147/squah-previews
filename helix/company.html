Maya Okafor

Co-founder & CEO

Maya spent years as a PM watching launches stall while she waited on the data team for a number she could already see in her head. After one too many tickets that came back a week late and answered the wrong question, she built Helix so product people could answer their own product questions — in minutes, not sprints.

She co-founded Helix in 2020 and runs it remote-first, with a team spread across 9 time zones.

The bet under the hood was deliberate: Helix is built on a purpose-built columnar event store, not bolted onto a generic OLAP database — which is why funnels and retention come back in milliseconds instead of minutes.
