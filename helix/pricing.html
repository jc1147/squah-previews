Pricing questions, answered

Do I pay per seat or per event?
Team and Business are priced per seat with a monthly event allowance; Enterprise is a custom contract with unlimited seats.
Can I start on Free and upgrade later?
Yes — every plan upgrades in place with no re-instrumentation, so your existing events, funnels and dashboards carry straight over.
Is the Free plan really free?
Yes. Up to 1M events a month, one project, and core funnels and retention — no credit card, no time limit, community support.
What security do paid plans include?
Every paid plan inherits SOC 2 Type II controls; Business adds SSO/SAML and an audit log, and Enterprise adds a HIPAA BAA and custom data residency.
