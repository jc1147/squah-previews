Questions

Questions about moving to SGEN

Do I need a developer to move to SGEN? +
No. On the white-glove path we rebuild the whole site for you, so you need no developer at all, you just review and approve. On the DIY path, SG Builder is visual drag-and-drop and the Theme Editor handles styling globally, so there is no code to write there either. Either way, moving to SGEN does not require a developer.
Will my content transfer, or do I retype everything? +
Your content comes across. There is no one-click WordPress importer, but you do not retype your site: export your pages and posts to CSV or JSON, then bring them in with SGEN's Advanced Import, which includes a visual field-mapping step that lines your columns up to SGEN fields and preserves draft and published status. The design is rebuilt rather than imported, and that is the deliberate part, it is how you leave the plugin stack and theme debt behind. On the white-glove path we handle the import for you.
How long does a rebuild take? +
It depends on the size of your site. For a white-glove rebuild, the timeline is scoped with you up front, so talk to sales. For a DIY rebuild, you set the pace: start free on the Sandbox today and launch whenever you are ready, with no time limit on the free tier.
What happens to my Google rankings when the URLs change? +
They follow you, as long as you set up redirects before launch. SGEN's native Redirects module lets you map every old URL to its new one with 301 (permanent) rules, and the Test URL dry-run confirms each rule matches before you go live. Map old to new, test, launch, and your link equity transfers with your rebuild. On the white-glove path we wire the redirects for you. See redirects for the full module.
What will it cost to be on SGEN after the rebuild? +
SGEN is one all-inclusive platform priced by how many live sites you run. Every plan includes the whole platform, all 23 modules, with nothing to add on. The free Sandbox runs everything on unlimited sandbox sites (staging URL only). Paid plans are Indie (1 site) at $39/mo annual ($47 monthly), Studio (3 sites) at $97/$117, Scale (10 sites) at $297/$333, and Enterprise (unlimited sites) at $497/$577. Enterprise is the published-price ceiling, not a sales call. There is a 2-week trial and you can cancel anytime, prorated. The white-glove rebuild itself is a separate, sales-quoted service.
