The cluster is ~3,800 searches/month — and you rank for none of it.

The intent split decides how you win it:

  • "interactive display" → touchscreens. Top results are SmartBoards, ViewSonic, BenQ, Samsung, LG — interactive whiteboards / flat panels. Chasing the bare term head-on is fighting Dell and Samsung for the wrong buyer.
  • "interactive 3D display" → your lane. Top results are HYPERVSN (#1), Looking Glass (#3), Samsung — your actual rivals, on a "world's-first interactive 3D holographic display" framing. You belong here, and you're absent.

The play — own the interactive 3D angle. Position your glasses-free displays as the interactive, no-glasses option and target: interactive 3D display, interactive digital signage ($23 CPC — high commercial intent), and the near-uncontested interactive retail display (KD 0) and interactive video wall (KD 6). You capture interactive demand by differentiating on 3D instead of losing to touchscreen panels.

  • Ads: strong commercial CPCs ($5.66–$23.17); interactive digital signage ($23) and interactive display ($12) are ready Google Ads targets once the pages exist.
  • Local: "interactive display [city]" is ~zero volume — this is a national play, not a localized-page one.
  • Competitor: the "interactive 3D display" SERP is owned by HYPERVSN and Looking Glass — the same rivals beating you elsewhere.
  • AI search: asked for the best interactive 3D display / digital-signage companies, ChatGPT names HYPERVSN, Looking Glass, and Samsung — not you (the opposite of "glasses-free 3D," where it names you first). Interactive-3D content closes that gap on Google and AI.

10Priorities — what's most needed, and why

  1. Recover wasted authority. Resolve the /oakes-farms/ page so your domain strength points at your products.
  2. Take the low-difficulty money terms — fast. Holographic signage (KD 0), autostereoscopic display (KD 2), holographic display (KD 26): real volume, you're absent, and they're nearly uncontested in difficulty.
  3. Build the hologram content cluster. The 74k/mo "holographic/hologram" informational terms are the leader's whole engine — and open to you.
  4. Win the signage lane with comparison + use-case pages that out-position the signage software platforms (BrightSign/Pickcel/Rise Vision).
  5. Fix the foundation: CDN + HTTP/2/3 to cut the 2.1-second LCP (most of it is server TTFB); clean duplicate meta + image alt; earn editorial links to dilute the footer-anchor profile.
  6. Protect and grow the AI-visibility lead — you're already cited in ChatGPT; make that durable while competitors aren't looking.
  7. Scale the footprint: 139 keywords → hundreds, via the content + localized-page engine in the strategy.
  8. Close the backlink gap with digital PR. 377 referring domains vs Looking Glass's 2,328 (~6×) — earn editorial links and dilute the footer-anchor profile. A sustained effort the content engine feeds, not a one-off.
  9. Point the localized pages at "trade show display [city]" (real volume + high CPC) rather than "glasses-free 3D [city]" (near-zero) — the live data picks the winning phrasing.
