Common questions

Quick answers.

Do you take my insurance?

Most likely. We're in-network with Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, Guardian, Humana, BCBS, Ameritas and most local plans. If you have a different plan, bring the card. We'll run the eligibility check before your appointment so there are no surprise bills.

How fast can I get an appointment?

For cleanings and checkups, usually within the week. For cracked teeth, toothaches, or anything that hurts, we hold same-day slots every weekday morning. Call the office and we'll get you in the chair today.

Do you see kids?

Yes. Dr. Elena Reyes is our board-certified pediatric dentist. She sees patients ages 1 through 17 on-site, no separate office across town. Spanish and English. Cavity-free club is real and the stickers are good.

Same-day crowns? Really?

Yes, really. We mill crowns in-house with a CEREC unit. Most crowns are done in one visit, usually 90 to 120 minutes start to finish. No temporary crown, no second appointment, no waiting two weeks while a lab makes it.

What if I haven't been to a dentist in a long time?

You're in good company. About a third of our new patients haven't been in over five years. No judgment, no lecture. We'll do a thorough exam, walk you through what we see, and put together a no-pressure plan. You decide what gets done and when.
