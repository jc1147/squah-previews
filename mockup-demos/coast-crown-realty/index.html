Off-market explained.

Sellers list with us privately when they want to avoid the public listing process — usually because of timing, privacy, or because they're testing the market. We send those listings to a small group of qualified buyers who match. You see them 7-14 days before they'd hit MLS (if they ever hit MLS at all).

Usually 5-10 days. We don't send anything that doesn't match your stated criteria — better to be empty than noisy. If your criteria are very specific (e.g. oceanfront bluff, 4+ bedrooms, under $3M), it can be longer between matches.
