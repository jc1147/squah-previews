HubSpot's lifecycle stages are designed for top-of-funnel demand-gen at higher volume / lower value. Quill is built for B2B SaaS account-level motion — buying committees, behavioral signal, and sales handoff that preserves context. Most customers run both: HubSpot for marketing-hub workflows, Quill for the account-and-handoff layer.
Average time-to-first-flow is 4 weeks across our existing customer base. Day 1: OAuth connection + initial sync. Week 1: behavioral signal model trained on your historical data. Week 2-3: first 2-3 flows shipped + sales handoff routing live. Week 4: first AE-routed accounts converting.
