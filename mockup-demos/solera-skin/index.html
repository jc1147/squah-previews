Our philosophy

Skincare that does what it says, and stops there.

Solera was started by a chemist who got tired of routines with twelve steps. The clean-formula philosophy is simple: every ingredient on every label, no proprietary blends hiding, no claims that need fine print, no fragrance unless it does something.

Fifty products. Each one for a reason. Made for skin. Sold by chemists. Returned never.

Founder · Lauren Whitaker

Photographed in real bathrooms. Tested in real ones.

Lauren Whitaker spent twelve years formulating for legacy beauty before walking out. The pitch she got tired of hearing — "we'll add this hot ingredient because everyone else is" — became the thing Solera explicitly refuses to do.

Every Solera formula starts with a single question: what is this trying to do, and is there evidence it does it? If the answer is hand-wavy, the formula doesn't ship. That's why the catalog tops out at fifty products. Each one is there because skin needs that exact thing.
