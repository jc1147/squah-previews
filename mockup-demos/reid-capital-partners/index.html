The approach

Three commitments. Printed in the engagement letter.

We've said no to commissions, kickbacks, and proprietary products since 2016. The structure isn't a marketing line — it's why the math works.

Fee-only.

One flat advisory fee, billed quarterly, disclosed in writing before you sign anything. Zero commissions. Zero kickbacks. Zero revenue-share from fund companies. The number we publish is the only number you'll see.

Fee-only · the only money we make from you, comes from you.

Fiduciary.

A legal obligation to act in your interest. We sign the CFP Board fiduciary oath. We publish the conflict-of-interest policy. We tell you when something we recommend has an alternative you should consider.

Fiduciary · the obligation goes one way — and it's toward you.

Independent.

No parent company. No quotas. No sales managers. We don't answer to Schwab, Vanguard, BlackRock, or anyone with a product to push. Custody sits at independent third parties so your money never touches our balance sheet.

Independent · the only seat at the table is yours.
