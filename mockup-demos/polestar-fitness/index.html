THE POLESTAR METHOD

45 minutes. Three pillars. Measured every week.

Built on compound strength, metabolic conditioning, and structured recovery. The method works because the load progresses and the data tells you.

01

Compound strength

Deadlift, squat, press, pull. Heavy load delivered safely, scaled to where you are, progressed weekly.

02

Metabolic conditioning

Short, hard intervals — sled push, kettlebell, rowing. Built to bend your engine, not break it.

03

Structured recovery

Sauna, cold plunge, percussive therapy on-site. Recovery is part of training, not the afterthought.
