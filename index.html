Common Questions

The objections we get the most.

How long does the build actually take?

Five business days for the five-page base build, and it scales from there: even a heavy 50-100 page site or a 200-product store is about two weeks, with brand-new-from-scratch builds taking a little longer. Faster if your content is ready when we start; slower only if you delay sending copy, images, or feedback (the clock pauses on customer delays over one business day). The 5-day guarantee applies to the five-page base, from kickoff, not from booking.

What if I need more than 5 pages later?

Each additional page is $100, flat. Two business days per added page. No project re-quote, no agency back-and-forth. You ask for a page, we add a page, you pay the page rate. There's no cap.

Why does the Ecom package start at $850 instead of $500?

Ecom Starter is $850 because it includes the SGEN Ecommerce module setup: product catalog, Stripe payment gateway wired, cart, checkout, and order-confirmation emails. That's $350 of additional configured work on top of the Basic $500 5-page build. Equivalent WordPress + WooCommerce builds run $1,500–$3,500 and require a $200+/mo retainer to keep alive. Ecom Pro at $1,500 adds PayPal, reviews, customer accounts, coupons, and abandoned-cart emails.

What's the Dutchie partnership about, and does it matter for my build?

Dutchie is the largest ecommerce + POS platform for retail cannabis in North America — 6,500+ dispensaries doing $15B+ in annual sales. SGEN is the technology and integration partner Dispenza (our cannabis-focused agency, 500+ dispensary locations on SGEN) uses to build and manage those sites. For your build, this means: if SGEN handles the compliance, traffic, and integration demands of cannabis retail at that scale, it can absolutely handle your local biz or ecom site.

Can I bring my own domain?

Yes. Point your existing domain at SGEN, we handle the DNS setup. If you don't have one, register one separately (most domains run $12–$15 a year at GoDaddy or Namecheap). That's not part of the $500.

What's NOT included in the $500?

Domain registration, paid stock-image licenses (we use free stock by default), the marketing add-ons listed above, and a branding kit if you don't have one yet. Everything you need for a live, working, 5-page site on SGEN is in the $500.

Does SGEN cost something monthly after the build?

Yes, and it's the best part. Every build includes a free first month on the platform, every package. After that, the SGEN platform is $19/mo: hosting, CMS, visual builder, and every module, one bill. That single $19 replaces the entire WordPress stack (hosting + plugins + maintenance retainer) that runs $200–500/mo, forever. Your site is hosted on SGEN, which is what keeps it fast, secure, and maintenance-free.

Do I own my design?

Yes. The design we create for you is yours, and you can use it anywhere you like, on or off SGEN. The live site itself is built and hosted on SGEN, so it isn't a file you download and move to another host. That's exactly what keeps it fast, secure, and maintenance-free: you own the look, we run the engine.

Is this WordPress underneath?

No. SGEN is a from-scratch platform. Not WordPress with a skin. Not WordPress with a managed-hosting layer. A different platform. That's the whole point.

What's the conversion tool I get? Form? Booking?

Default is a contact form wired straight to your inbox via GoHighLevel. If your business needs a calendar booking instead (consultants, service businesses, healthcare), upgrade to the Booking Form add-on at checkout for $100 one-time.
