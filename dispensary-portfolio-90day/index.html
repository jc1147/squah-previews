Why This Earns Your Signature

What you walk away with — and the questions your execs will ask

A 90-day pilot that front-loads the heavy lift, proves value before the full rate, and answers the two questions every executive team raises about a switch like this.

Exec question 1 — "How will we rank in AI / LLM platforms (and what about Bing)?"

Answer engines don't rank search positions — they surface authoritative, authentic third-party signals (community discussion, press, "best of" lists). Bing is secondary. Our move is to measure assistant-referred traffic first (it's currently untracked), then build the signals that earn citations. You get a real number, not a claim.

Exec question 2 — "We've been told our site and listings are in terrible shape. Which is it?"

Two different surfaces, two different answers. Local is strong — our 60+ grid scans show #1–#3 across the cluster terms that carry the volume (the only soft spot is the "marijuana" term, normal for the category). Classic organic has slipped — first-page rankings are well off their 12-month peak, which the rebuild + recovery roadmap is built to reverse. We hand you the scan evidence and the ranking-radius definitions so the picture is objective, not anecdotal.
