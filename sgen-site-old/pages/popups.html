The whole popup system, native

Control exactly when, where, and how often each popup shows.

A popup is only as good as its targeting, show it to the wrong visitor, too often, and it costs you the conversion it was meant to win. SGEN configures every one from the editor, with the controls that actually move the number.

Autoload & frequency

Fire automatically on page load, or leave Autoload off. Set a day count so a returning visitor sees the same offer on a cadence, once a week, say, instead of on every single visit.

Click-to-open

Every popup has a hashcode. Point any menu item, button, or inline link at it and the click opens that exact popup on demand, a "Get the guide" button that pops the signup form, not just an auto-interruption.

Page targeting

Scope a popup to specific pages or post types instead of the whole site. A cart-page cross-sell that never appears on the blog. The targeting that justified a SaaS subscription, included.

First-time visitors

Limit a popup to people who haven't already seen and dismissed it, for a welcome or first-touch offer that never nags a returning reader.

Dismissal controls

Disable the close button, disable click-outside-to-close, or disable stacking so two popups never fight for the screen at once. You decide how the overlay behaves.

Per-popup styling

Override the default width and attach a custom CSS class to style one popup differently from the rest. Drop in a form from the Forms module by shortcode and its submissions land in the same inbox.

Frequently asked questions about Popups

Which trigger types are supported? +
Popups can Autoload on page load, with a frequency setting that controls how often a returning visitor sees them again, or open on click by pointing any link or button at the popup's hashcode. You can also limit a popup to first-time visitors and scope it to specific pages or post types.
Can I target a popup to specific pages only? +
Yes. Popup configuration includes page targeting, show on all pages, or scope to specific pages or post types. A popup set for only the cart page will not appear on your blog.
Can I stop a popup showing to the same visitor over and over? +
Two ways. "Show only to first-time visitors" hides the popup from anyone who has already seen and dismissed it. The Autoload frequency setting controls the cadence, for example, once every seven days, for returning visitors who haven't dismissed it permanently.
Can a popup contain a form from the Forms module? +
Yes. Build the form in the Forms module, copy its shortcode, and paste it into the popup's Content (HTML) field. The form renders inside the popup and its submissions land in the Forms submissions inbox as normal, two native modules in one platform that just work together.
Can a popup open from a button or menu link instead of on page load? +
Yes. Turn Autoload off, then use the popup's hashcode (shown in the editor after you save) as the href of a menu item, button, or inline link. Clicking it opens that specific popup on demand.
Can I design popups visually instead of writing HTML? +
The editor offers an SG Builder option for visual, drag-and-drop popup design. It's currently in beta, the verified-stable path today is the standard Text Editor (HTML), so reach for that when a popup needs to ship reliably.

Every plan. Popups is one of the 22 native modules, and every plan is the whole platform, plans differ only by how many live sites you run.
