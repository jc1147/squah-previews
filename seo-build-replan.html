working

03 What we did out of order

We skipped the page thesis + intent/winnability validation

Your locked logic was analyze → form the target thesis → VALIDATE → opportunities → competition, per page. When the live SERP sweep kept hanging, the reliable pivot (_quickplan) did a thin thesis — slug-derived candidates, pick the highest volume — and dropped the gate that asks "can this page credibly win this, and is the SERP even the right page type?" Everything downstream (competitor R&D, content map) was then built on those focuses, so wherever a focus is mis-targeted, the downstream inherits the error.

PROOF — product/forms: focus = "online form builder" (vol 9,900, KD 71). That phrase appears 0× on the page; the SERP is free-tool dominated ("Free Online Forms", Google Forms, Tally) but the page is a paid $39/mo module. Right target ≠ the highest-volume slug term.

Blast radius — sized from the data, not guessed. It is a subset, not everything:
