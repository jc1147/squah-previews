Before you give

Donor questions, answered.

Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes. Riverkeep Alliance is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, and all gifts are tax-deductible to the full extent of the law. You will receive an emailed receipt for your records as soon as your gift is processed.
Where does my donation actually go?
92 cents of every dollar funds programs — cleanups, plantings, monitoring, and education. The remaining 8 cents covers audited operations and fundraising, so the watershed work keeps running year after year.
What is a monthly River Guardian?
A River Guardian gives a sustaining monthly gift that funds year-round restoration. At $25 a month, your giving plants a mile of streambank buffer over the course of a year — and you can pause or change it anytime.
Can I give stock or leave a gift in my will?
Absolutely. Legacy and planned giving — a bequest in your will or a gift of appreciated stock to the River Forever Fund — protects the river beyond your lifetime. Reach out and we will walk you and your advisor through it.
