Our story

From a bootstrapped brand to the line other brands run on.

The brand we built first is the whole reason BatchWorks exists: a premium pre-roll brand built the hard way, the wall every brand eventually hits, and the decision to build past it.

Day one

A conviction, and not much else

BatchWorks started with two cousins, a conviction about what makes for a good smoke, and not much else. Bootstrapped, no backing, just a clear point of view about what a good product should be.

2018

A Nevada first

We built a premium pre-roll brand the hard way. It was called Hunter & Leaf, and was the first brand in Nevada to offer hemp-wrapped blunts, flavored wraps, glass tips, and premium glass packaging.

The wall

The part you can’t out-hustle

Growing the brand exposed the real problem. There is no single process or recipe that makes every pre-roll well, and consistency at volume is the wall between a good brand and a scalable one.

The pivot

That truth became BatchWorks

Solving consistency at scale turned into its own company. We’ve been in your seat. We built this so you don’t have to.
