The questions
everyone asks.

Pick an objection. We answer it with the receipts, not the spin.

Answer 01 — Migration

No. Our import maps your pages, posts, and media into SG-Core automatically, and you preview the whole site on staging before anything goes live. Most single sites move in an afternoon; agencies batch-migrate portfolios in a weekend.

  1. Importpages · posts · media mapped
  2. Stagingpreview the full site
  3. Verifylinks · redirects · forms
  4. Go liveone deploy
0afternoonsingle-site move

Answer 02 — No lock-in

It's an alternative. The no-prison policy means your content and code are exportable at any time. You're choosing SGEN because it's faster and cheaper, not because you can't leave.

Content & mediaExport · any time
Source & markupExport · any time
Database & redirectsExport · any time
0portableno-prison policy

Answer 03 — Price

WP Engine charges for the platform and makes you buy plugins for the builder, forms, SEO, and compliance separately. SGEN bundles those as suites priced per site, so the all-in cost runs 18–43% lower across comparable plans.

Answer 04 — Native modules

The features most plugins provide — forms, SEO, ecommerce, analytics, popups, media management — ship as native SG-Modules. No plugin to update, no conflict to debug. If you need something bespoke, the platform is extensible.
