12 · Iconography

One grid, one weight, one family.

SGEN icons are Lucide-style line marks — a single 24×24 viewBox, drawn in currentColor with exactly one stroke weight. They inherit the colour of their text context, scale on a four-step ramp, and sit in one chip recipe. Every glyph below is rendered live, inline, with zero external dependencies — and every one is harvested from icons the demos already ship.

1The spec, in four numbers

The non-negotiables. Authored on a single grid, one optical line, soft round terminals, colour inherited — never hard-coded. Full text in _ICON-SYSTEM.md.

viewBox

Single canonical grid. Lucide / Feather native. ~2px keyline trim → 20×20 live area.
stroke
currentColor
Never a hex on the SVG. The icon takes the colour of its text context and re-themes for free.
stroke-width
1.8
THE one weight — fixed across all sizes. One emphasis exception: 2.4 on the scroll-top button.
joins / caps
round
Soft round terminals everywhere. No mitred corners — matches the BeTheme settle aesthetic.

2The canonical icon — copy this, swap the paths

Every SGEN icon is this exact markup with a different inner path. The dashed frame is the 24-unit grid; the inner square is the ~20-unit live area Lucide draws inside.

24 × 24 grid ~20 live area

<svg class="icon" viewBox="0 0 24 24"
     fill="none" stroke="currentColor"
     stroke-width="1.8"
     stroke-linecap="round"
     stroke-linejoin="round"
     aria-hidden="true">
  <path d="M20 6L9 17l-5-5"/>
</svg>

4The icon-chip recipe

When an icon needs a ground it sits in a square chip on a 10% accent tint with a hairline accent border — the single chip used across the feature tiles. Hover a card and the chip lifts −2px with it. Four chip sizes, plus inverted and solid variants.

7Decorative vs meaningful — the two patterns

Most icons sit beside a text label and are aria-hidden. Icon-only buttons keep the glyph hidden and put the label on the button, with a ≥44×44 target and a visible focus ring. Tab to the buttons below to see the ring.

Decorative (the default)

The adjacent text is the label. The icon is aria-hidden="true" and reinforces — never replaces — the words. Meaning is never colour-only.
