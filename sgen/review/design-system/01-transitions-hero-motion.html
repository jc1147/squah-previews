A

Section transitions & shape dividers

How one band meets the next — SVG shapes where the mood fits, and the equally-premium color-band / hairline / image-fade alternatives where they don't.

⚠ §0.5 — SVG shape dividers are NOT universal (per-vertical, opt-in)

The harvest does not uniformly support dividers as a global move. Use them where the vertical's mood fits — organic = waves/hills/blobs, techy = tilt/triangle/split, events/music = wave/slant. Where they don't (fashion, luxury real-estate, finance) reach for color-band contrast + whitespace + a hairline rule + the image bottom-fade instead. Discipline regardless: one divider family per page, used 2–4× — never mix wave + triangle + blob.
