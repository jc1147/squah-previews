02

Display face on headings + oversized, tight, negative-tracked type

Typography · the #1 typographic lever
15/16
FLATALIVE
FLAT = mid-size grotesk, normal tracking. ALIVE = serif display, oversized, light, negative tracking.

Built for teams who ship.

Light weight at huge size reads luxury; line-height:~1.0 and letter-spacing:-.04em tighten the whole headline into one confident block.

How to build  Pair ONE expressive display face (serif: Fraunces / Playfair; or condensed: Bebas / Syne) for H1–H3 with a neutral grotesk body. Push the hero to clamp(48px,7vw,90–142px), line-height:~1.0, letter-spacing:-.02em to -.04em, weight 300–500.

Lift on hover

How to build  Resting: box-shadow:0 15px 30px 0 rgba(1,7,39,.13) (the single most-recurring verbatim shadow in the harvest — navy-tinted, not black). Hover: translateY(-6px) + 0 32px 64px -12px rgba(16,24,40,.18) over .25s cubic-bezier(.05,.5,.3,1). Negative spread tucks the shadow under the card.

06

Glass sticky header — transparent over hero → blurred on scroll

Depth + motion · scroll the box
13/16
FLATALIVE
FLAT = solid opacity:.97 fill (the BeTheme default). ALIVE = real backdrop-filter glass. Scroll inside the box.
◆ AcmeProductPricingDocs
Transparent over the hero

Scroll down — past the hero, the header gains a translucent fill and a real blur, shrinks its padding, and picks up a hairline border + soft shadow.

Most BeTheme demos ship a flat opaque opacity:.97 bar. Real backdrop-filter glass is a free upgrade over the source — toggle FLAT to compare the stuck state.

The header stays pinned while this content slides beneath it.

Keep scrolling to confirm the stuck state holds.

The glass reads "expensive" because it samples and blurs the live content behind it.

How to build  Header position:fixed; background:transparent over the hero. On scroll past the hero add .is-stuck → translucent fill + backdrop-filter:blur(18px) saturate(180%) + hairline border + soft shadow, shrink padding. CSS + tiny scroll listener. Always provide a solid-bg fallback for browsers without backdrop-filter.

13/16
FLATALIVE
FLAT = static, fades to opacity:.9. ALIVE = fill ↔ ghost invert. Hover the buttons.

How to build  No static buttons. Solid-fill → transparent-with-accent-text, or ghost → fills with accent, over transition:background-color .2s, color .2s, border-color .2s (named props, NOT transition:all or opacity:.9). Pick ONE radius language site-wide: pill 9999px, sharp 0px + tracked caps (luxe), or 6–10px (fintech).

12

Ambient depth behind the hero (glow blobs / duotone wash)

Depth · the "AI startup" look in ~6 lines
12/16
FLATALIVE
FLAT = flat near-black hero. ALIVE = blurred radial glow blobs in the accent, slowly breathing behind the dark hero.

Premium, alive — not flat.

Two offset glow blobs behind a near-black hero ≈ the whole tech-startup look.

How to build  Absolutely-positioned ::before/::after (or spans): radial-gradient(circle, accent .55, transparent 70%); filter:blur(55px); z-index:0, behind a near-black hero. Slow-animate position for "breathing." Alternatives: a soft 135° duotone wash, or gentle transform parallax (gated, throttled).
