The discipline

Wow should be felt, not noticed.

The bar is not "clean and flat" — it is premium and alive: the subtle depth, motion, texture and light that read high-end. The premium signal is what you leave out, not what you pile on. Every demo here obeys the same governing rule that recurred in all sixteen category files — ruthless restraint.

One accent

A single saturated hue carries every CTA, link, counter and rule. Everything else is tinted ink on off-white paper — never #000, never #fff.

One display face

One expressive face for headings, one neutral grotesk for body. Oversized, tight, negative-tracked — light-at-large reads luxe.

One shadow family

The universal ink-tinted soft shadow 0 15px 30px rgba(1,7,39,.13) at rest, deepening on a −4px lift. Tint toward the ink, never pure black.

One motion easing

The BeTheme settle cubic-bezier(.05,.5,.3,1) on reveals, a fast confident .2s on hover — and the whole of it gated behind reduced-motion.
