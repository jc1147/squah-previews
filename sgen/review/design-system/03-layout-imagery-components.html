Category H

Imagery, masks & frames

Every product / gallery / team / blog image is a clipped, framed, zoom-able object. One masked focal shape per page; the rest stay clean consistent-radius rectangles. (Visuals here are CSS-crafted gradients — no external images by design.)

H1Clipped frame · zoom + exposure hover
15/16

How: .frame{overflow:hidden} + img{transition:transform .45s} → scale(1.08) on hover; dark .mask fades in, caption rises (translateY) and an underline grows width 0→40px.

H2Shape-masked imagery

H6Image bottom-fade into next section

Category I

Component polish

No static buttons, no flat cards. Token-driven invert buttons, lifting cards, count-up stats, glass-on-scroll headers, live sliders and accordions — the tactile layer that reads "expensive."
