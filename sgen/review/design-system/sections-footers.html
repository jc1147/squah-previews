Sections — Footers · zone 8 · the page close

Footers

Four production-grade footer section blocks — each a complete <footer> you can drop into a real page, not a description of one. They sit at zone 8 of the composition spine (S-FOOTER): the dark-panel close that carries secondary nav, contact recap, social, trust and legal. Built on the SGEN chrome — charcoal #1a1a1a, red #d51522, one 8 px button radius, the ink-tinted shadow family. Every block is responsive (columns→stack at 767 per the responsive library, legal row centers, back-to-top in a 20 px gutter) and copy-ready. CSS-crafted visuals, system fonts, zero external dependencies, motion gated behind prefers-reduced-motion.

Live blocks — hover links, focus the newsletter input, click subscribe / back-to-top. Each block shows a when to use caption + its pattern IDs.

A · Mega footer

multi-column + newsletter

When to use: content-dense sites (SaaS, ecommerce, enterprise, marketplaces) where the footer doubles as a sitemap — you have 4+ link groups, want a newsletter capture, and need a payment/social/legal close all in one panel.

Composition S-FOOTER (zone 8) · Look J1 dark panel · I10 payment strip · I8 back-to-top · Responsive F1 cols→stack@767 (3-up@768–959) · F2 legal centers · F4 gutter

B · Standard columns

brand + link columns + contact

When to use: the everyday default — service businesses, clinics, firms, B2B sites. A brand column with a contact recap (address / phone / email / hours) plus three link columns. Enough structure to orient, not a full sitemap.

Composition S-FOOTER (zone 8) · Look dark anchor · contact recap · A3 hairline columns · Responsive F1 4-col→3-up@960→stack@767 · F2 legal centers
