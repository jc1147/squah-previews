Questions

The things teams ask before they switch

Do I need an engineer to publish?
No. The whole point is that marketing owns the surface. A non-engineer edits a pricing line or ships a landing page in the browser — no repo, no build, no deploy window. Engineering stays on the product.
What happens to our existing SEO and traffic?
The redirect manager maps every legacy URL with a 301, so your hard-won organic equity carries through the rebuild intact. On-page SEO — meta, Open Graph, canonicals, JSON-LD, and sitemap — is edited inline on each page, so metadata can't drift out of sync with content.
Is this really one bill, or a base price plus add-ons?
One bill. Hosting, SSL, a global CDN, a WAF, and automatic patching are part of the Foundation Pack on every plan — including the free Sandbox. There's no plugin tax and no suite tax: the price you see is what you pay.
Can I see which content actually drives demos?
Yes — that's the attribution capability. First-party UTM with first-touch, last-touch, and multi-touch tracking ties each demo request back to the post, channel, and campaign that produced it, all on one screen.
How do I try it without committing?
Start on the $0 Sandbox and prototype the full platform — no card, no time limit. When you're ready for production, Launch is $17/mo, all-inclusive.
