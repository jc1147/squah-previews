The firm

The lawyer you reach is the lawyer you keep.

At a big firm your matter is handed down a chain. Here it isn't. When you call Carrow & Bly, you reach a partner — and that same partner drafts your documents, walks you through them at the kitchen-table level, and is still the one who picks up two years later when something changes.

We built this practice on the belief that the most important legal decisions are also the most personal. A will, a family business, the care of an aging parent — these deserve patience and plain language, not a meter running. Most of our clients come to us by referral, and most stay for a generation.
