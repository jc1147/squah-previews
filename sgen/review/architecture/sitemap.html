Four WordPress pains — four native fixes

Bleeding Traffic

Server-side rendering delivers finished content to users — and Google — instantly.
PAIN 02

Broken UX

"Most sites choke on background scripts. To convert, content must render the moment a user clicks."

1

Pick a base plan

  • Sandbox $0 — free forever, 1K visits, subdomain
  • Launch $17 · Grow $42 · Scale $89 · Multi $199/mo (Launch = popular)
  • Enterprise — contact sales (custom, sales-quoted; no public price)
  • 2-week trial · founder pricing locks in for life
2

Add module suites

  • Add Suites — the 21 modules grouped into 7 Suites
  • Marketing/SEO · Performance Pro · Ecommerce
  • Content&Dev · Multi-Location · Agency · Compliance
  • 5-Suite Bundle — save ~30% (sliding ~$63–80/site/mo)
3

Review & checkout

  • See your stack + total before paying
  • Scale modules up/down anytime
  • One bill, one platform — no plugin invoices
