Made on SGEN

Every site here was built on one stack, no plugins.

This is a showcase that doubles as a product demo: the work is the platform. Each site was designed, built, and shipped on SGEN's visual builder and native modules, all in one place.

One builder, no code

Every site was assembled in the visual, drag-and-drop builder from a finished template, not a blank page. The same builder runs on the free Sandbox and on every paid plan.

The add-ons are in the box

Forms, popups, SEO, ecommerce, attribution, backups, and consent are native modules, not licensed add-ons. The stack collapse you see is a stitched third-party setup replaced by one platform.

Free baseline under all of it

Hosting, SSL, CDN, WAF, auto-patching, and the multi-site dashboard ship on every plan, down to the free Sandbox. That is why these sites stay fast and stay up without a maintenance treadmill.
