Beyond page views

Squarespace tells you who visited. SGEN tells you what paid for them.

If you are running paid campaigns, the number that matters is not visits, it is which channel, ad, and keyword drove the conversion. Built-in website analytics typically answer the traffic question and stop there.

SGEN ships attribution as a native module: multi-touch attribution and call tracking, built into the platform. No CallRail subscription, no third-party tag to maintain, no separate line item. For an operator with a marketing budget, that is the difference between knowing traffic and knowing return.

See what worked, not just who came. For paid campaigns, attribute the conversion to the channel, ad, and keyword that drove it.
Built in, not bolted on. Multi-touch attribution lives in the platform, not in another monthly subscription you have to maintain.

Questions

SGEN vs Squarespace, answered

Can I move my Squarespace site over to SGEN? +
Moving from Squarespace is a rebuild, not an import, your content does not transfer automatically. You rebuild on SGEN using the visual builder and templates, or we rebuild it for you (white-glove). Both paths are on the migrate page.
Is SGEN harder to use than Squarespace? +
The SGEN builder is visual and drag-and-drop, the same editing model. The difference is the ceiling: SGEN ships 22 native modules without an add-on tier, so capability you would otherwise grow into is already present. If you have built a Squarespace site, you can build a SGEN site.
Does SGEN charge a transaction fee when I sell? +
No. There is no SGEN-imposed transaction fee on any plan, and ecommerce is a native module included in every plan. By contrast, Squarespace's Basic plan charges a 2% commerce transaction fee (Core, Plus, and Advanced charge none).
How does SGEN pricing compare to Squarespace? +
SGEN starts at $39/mo (billed annually; $47 monthly), and the Foundation Pack, hosting, SSL, CDN, WAF, and security patching, is free on every plan, including the free Sandbox. The meaningful comparison is what is included, plus no SGEN transaction fee on sales. Squarespace prices are not shown here; verify them on live squarespace.com.
Is there a free trial? +
SGEN includes a free Sandbox with no trial expiry, open an account and build on unlimited sandbox sites (staging URL only) at no cost. Paid plans add a 2-week trial. Cancel anytime, prorated.
