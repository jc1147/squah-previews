No. 07
Questions

Common questions about switching from GoDaddy

Can I move my GoDaddy site over to SGEN? +
Moving from GoDaddy is a rebuild, not an import: your content does not transfer automatically, because no website builder imports another builder's site. You rebuild on SGEN using the visual builder and templates, or we rebuild it for you (white-glove). Both paths are at /migrate, and the Redirects module maps every old URL to its new home so you keep your link equity.
Is SGEN harder to use than GoDaddy? +
GoDaddy is genuinely good at speed to a first site, and that simplicity is a real strength for a non-technical owner. SGEN's builder is also visual and drag-and-drop, the same editing model you would expect from a modern builder. The difference is the ceiling: SGEN ships 23 native modules without a feature-tier ladder, so the capabilities a growing site needs are already present, not unlocked plan by plan.
What can SGEN do that an entry website builder cannot? +
SGEN ships 23 native modules across 7 categories: custom content types, events, ecommerce, forms, popups, redirects, and native lead attribution, plus server-side rendering and a multi-site dashboard, all on one platform and on every plan. It is built for the stage where a brochure site is no longer enough, the point where an entry builder starts gating capabilities to higher tiers.
How does SGEN pricing compare to GoDaddy? +
Honestly, GoDaddy's entry plan is cheaper at the sticker (about $10 to $13/mo billed annually, soft range, verify current pricing at godaddy.com). SGEN starts at $39/mo (billed annually; $47 monthly). What SGEN's price includes is the whole platform: all 23 modules and the Foundation Pack (hosting, SSL, CDN, WAF, security patching, multi-site dashboard), free on every plan including the $0 Sandbox. You pay by how many live sites you run, never by feature tier. The value is depth and ownership, not the entry price.
Is there a free trial? +
SGEN includes a free Sandbox with no trial expiry: open an account and build the whole platform on unlimited staging sites (staging URL only) at no cost. Paid plans add a 2-week trial. Cancel anytime, prorated.
